Churn Analytics

Determine propensity of customer churn

Researched & Explored patterns consistent with dormant customers in a bid to improve customer retention by understanding customer behavior and Identifying existing customers that are about to stop transacting with the business.

To do this, a supervised learning approach was used to distinguish the unique pattern of an active client against a dormant client with the objective of mitigating dormancy.

This was achieved by leveraging exhaustive data on customer's demographics, transactional data, Interactions data, & digital channels data from their respective internal databases.

This initiative was able to identify several customers with high potential to churn & who account for over N200bn in AUM to be engaged by the customer experience team.

Fraud Detection & Management Solution

Identify Suspicious transactions & flag it to business

This use case involved extensive research on the transactional behaviour of each of the customers. The objective was to automate the tracking and identification of suspicious transactions made by each customer in a bid to reduce the overall man-hours spent by the Fraud team in manually identifying and resolving these cases.

The general hypothesis was that each customer has a unique transactional pattern, and when there's an anomaly in the transactional pattern of a customer, then it should be flagged and escalated to the appropriate team for resolution.

This initiative leveraged unsupervised machine learning & deep learning models for anomaly detection, and was deployed as part of a fully scalable, end-to-end solution that is currently being adopted by one of the top financial institutions in Nigeria.

Data Migration Auditing

Validating completeness & accuracy, and ensuring a successful data integration

The objective of this project was to ensure a successful integration of data for a client during a merger. This involved validating completeness & accuracy of the data being wrangled at every point in time.

This initiative required querying of SQL databases, and leveraging big data frameworks (including, but not limited to PySpark, Hive) on Hadoop clusters to extract, transform, load, and test the client's data.

Sentiment Analysis

Analysing Twitter content with respect to our product, to improve Customer Experience & Acquisition

This project leveraged the concept of NLP to gain an overview of the wider public opinion behind certain topics in a bid to to identify potential customers, and to find out how people relate with our product as well as our competitors'.

This initiative required access to the Twitter API for the extraction of tweets containing the relevant keywords, and leads were sent to the customer experience team for further engagement upon completion of the analysis.
